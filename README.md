# Holy Bible in Markdown
# 🚀 Project Title

## Overview
When I first came into Github, I had an idea of putting the Holy Bible in a place where people can easily read it and use it in many projects for the Lord. 

I noticed that it already had many projects of that type, but I never found one that where more than one language or translation were included. 
So, I decided to fill the gap.

---

## Core Concept

At its core, this project is based on the famous Bible verse _Matthew 28:19-20_:

> Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you. And behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age.”

Thus, it is structured so that anyone from anywhere can easily read and hear God's Word. 

Not only people can people consume it, the project's structure also allows anyone to rapidly work in the harvest and contribute to the sharing of the Good News!
